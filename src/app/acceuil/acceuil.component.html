Welcome to our online asteroid library. Created in 2018, AsteroNet provides to Internet users informations about known and unknown asteroids (collected thanks to the NASA API). Why asteroids? It's very simple, passionate or not by astronomy, many of us are more or less familiar with the eight planets that make up our solar system. But know that space does not stop there! There are also the moons, the planets, the comets... And there are still more new galaxies to discover with, who knows, maybe other solar systems. But we stopped well before all these stellar trips to bring you informations on asteroids, which are also part of our universe. First, we will use your data on the asteroids - the most known -, and then a page dedicated to daily news. You will be able to use all the information through our interactive interface, as if you were inside a spaceship with its super dashboard!

Project realized as part of the training Web / Mobile Developer at the Wild Code School
